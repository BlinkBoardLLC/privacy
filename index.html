BlinkBoard does NOT collect, store, transmit, share, or retain any personal information, biometric data, facial recognition data, eye tracking data, communication content, user behavior data, device information, or any other data about users.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how BlinkBoard handles data and protects your privacy. BlinkBoard is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all users, especially those who may be vulnerable or have communication needs.

2. What We Don't Collect

BlinkBoard does NOT collect, store, transmit, share, or retain:

  • Personal information
  • Biometric data
  • Facial recognition data
  • Eye tracking data
  • Communication content
  • User behavior data
  • Device information
  • Any other data about users

All processing happens locally on your device in real-time. No information ever leaves your device.

3. Camera and Face Tracking

BlinkBoard uses your device's front-facing camera exclusively for real-time eye blink detection to enable communication. The camera processing:

  • Occurs entirely on your device using Apple's ARKit framework
  • Does not save, store, or record any images, videos, or camera data
  • Does not create or store facial recognition profiles or biometric templates
  • Does not capture or retain any visual information about the user
  • Processes camera data in real-time memory only
  • Automatically discards all camera data immediately after processing
  • Does not transmit any visual information anywhere

The camera access is used solely for the functional purpose of detecting intentional eye blinks to enable communication and for no other purpose.

4. Local Data Processing

Any temporary data created during use (such as typed sentences) exists only in the device's active memory during the current session. This data:

  • Is not saved to device storage
  • Is not backed up to iCloud or any cloud service
  • Is not synchronized with any external service
  • Is automatically cleared when the app is closed or restarted
  • Cannot be recovered or accessed after the session ends
  • Is never transmitted outside the device

5. No Third-Party Services

BlinkBoard does not integrate with, connect to, or use any third-party services including:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks or platforms
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Social media platforms
  • Data collection or marketing services
  • External databases or servers
  • Artificial intelligence or machine learning services that require data transmission

Your privacy is completely protected from any external data collection or sharing.

6. Device Permissions

BlinkBoard only requests camera permission, which is necessary for eye blink detection. The app does not request or use:

  • Microphone access
  • Location services
  • Contacts or address book access
  • Photo library access
  • Network or internet connectivity for data transmission
  • Push notification services
  • Any other device permissions beyond camera access

7. Children's Privacy

BlinkBoard is designed to protect the privacy of users of all ages, including children. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there are no special data handling procedures required for younger users. However, caregivers must ensure they have appropriate permission to set up and supervise the use of this application with children.

8. Security

Since BlinkBoard processes all data locally and stores nothing, there are no data security risks related to external storage, transmission, or unauthorized access to user data. The security of the app depends on the security of your iOS device and your device's camera permissions.

9. Medical Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: BlinkBoard is NOT a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, prevent, or mitigate any disease, medical condition, or disability. This Application is provided for educational, assistive, and communication purposes only.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this webpage. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
